2,35 billion kg of CO2 were emitted by the US alone in 2021! This is the equivalent to 471,000 cars on the road per year. Unfortunately, our global emissions will keep increasing to satisfy the demands of the growing retail industry.
The information is gathered from Green America's "Skip the Slip" report.

In 2021 the US consumed 37,8 billion liters of water to manage and produce paper receipts. According to the UN, we can only use 0.5% of the water on Earth as freshwater. However, as our population grows the demand will greatly surpass our supply of useable water.
The information is gathered from Green America's "Skip the Slip" report.

Forests absorb around 40% of global fossil fuel emissions every year! As trees grow older, their capacity to absord carbon increases. That is why plating new trees after cutting the older ones, is not a very effective solution in the short term. Additionally, these old trees release all of the carbon they have stored when they are cut down and processed.
The information is gathered from Green America's "Skip the Slip" report.
